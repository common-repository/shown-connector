The Shown connector is used to sync data between WordPress, WooCommerce and Shown.

== Description ==
The Shown Connector simplifies your business setup on Shown.io, making your advertising more efficient. It automates ad tracking by installing the Shown pixel on your website and detecting key website actions (conversions). Additionally, it synchronizes customer emails to help Shown create better audiences, optimizing your ad performance. Finally, it syncs your products, enabling shopping ads across multiple platforms.

Data is exchanged with Shown.io under the [terms of service](https://shown.io/en/terms) and [privacy policy](https://shown.io/en/privacy). In the plugin settings, you can choose which data types to synchronize.

### What is Shown.io
Shown is an AI-powered advertising platform designed to make online advertising accessible to everyone. It simplifies the creation and management of cross-channel ad campaigns by utilizing integrations with platforms like WordPress, AI technology, and a dedicated team of ad experts committed to ensuring your success.

For more information, please visit [https://www.shown.io](https://www.shown.io).

### Shown pixel & conversion tracking
The Shown Pixel works similarly to Google Tag Manager, automatically injecting channel-specific tags like the Facebook Pixel and Google Ads tag on your website. This eliminates the need for manual setup for each platform.

Additionally, the Shown Connector enables automatic tracking of key conversions, such as completed purchases and add-to-cart actions.
These pixels and conversion tracking are essential for monitoring ad performance and optimizing your campaigns for more sales or leads.

Importantly, we do not store any metrics on our servers, ensuring full end-user data privacy and compliance with privacy regulations.

### Products synchronization
The store owner has full control over which products to synchronize with Shown, with the option to either sync all products at once or individually as they are created. These product lists are exclusively used for launching shopping campaigns on platforms like Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or any other channel supported by Shown.

### Customers synchronization
The store owner has full control over which customers (emails) to synchronize with Shown, with the option to either sync all customers at once or individually as they place new orders. This customer data is exclusively used for creating lookalike & retargeting audiences on platforms like Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or any other channel supported by Shown.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I use the Shown connector plugin without a shown.io account? =
No, you need to have a Shown account and link it to your WordPress website to take advantage of this plugin functionalities.
